Jot iOS — Record + Transcript · Variant 3

Liquid Glass tabbed · Maximalist iOS 26 expression

Thesis: The maximalist take. Floating Liquid Glass tab bar (Record / Library / Settings — only Record active in MVP). Glass mic capsule centered. History rendered as Liquid Glass cards, not list rows. Larger gestures, lusher surfaces, more chrome. The point is to put the full iOS 26 expression next to V1 so the team can ask: does this earn its complexity, or is V1 already enough?
