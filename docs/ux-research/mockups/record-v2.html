Jot iOS — Record + Transcript · Variant 2

Superwhisper morphing pill · One capsule walks every state

The transcript lands inside the same surface that just held the recording state, dwells ~1.5s, then dissolves into the top row of a stock list below. Monochrome ground, system blue as the only accent, restrained motion. Inspired by Superwhisper's Mac pill — translated for one-handed phone use.
