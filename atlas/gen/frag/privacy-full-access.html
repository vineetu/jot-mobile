Full Access.
Why the Jot keyboard asks for Full Access — and what it does with it.
What it's for
Full Access lets the keyboard read the transcript the main app produced, so it can paste your words into the app you're typing in. Without it, paste can't work.
Turn it on
Allow Full Access
General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Jot
↗
No status is shown here — iOS doesn't tell the app whether Full Access is on. This opens Jot's page in iOS Settings, where the toggle lives.
Your privacy is protected
Full Access only enables the keyboard's microphone so speech becomes text in real time. Nothing you say or type ever leaves your iPhone.
