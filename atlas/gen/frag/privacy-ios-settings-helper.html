Don't see "Keyboards" settings?
Close and reopen Settings, or open the Jot app and follow Settings → Privacy → Full Access (General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Jot → Allow Full Access).
Your privacy is protected
Nothing you say or type ever leaves your iPhone.* Full Access only enables the keyboard's microphone so your speech becomes text in real time. You can verify all of this in Apple's App Privacy Report.
* The one exception is the Send Feedback form in the app's Settings, which only sends feedback when you deliberately open it and send — never automatically.
