What happens during the warm window
iOS keeps the orange microphone indicator visible while the session stays warm. No audio is captured or retained during this window.
Warm Hold never blocks other apps. Take a call or join a meeting and Jot instantly steps aside, then quietly reclaims the warm session next time you dictate.
The same held-but-not-capturing behavior backs Pause / Resume — paused audio is dropped, never stored.
