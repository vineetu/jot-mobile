How do I pause a recording?
From Jot's Help
While a recording is in progress, tap the Pause button in the control cluster (to the left of the stop pill). Pausing holds the audio session open but stops capturing — the iOS orange mic indicator stays on, and any audio while paused is dropped, never stored.
Tap Resume to keep going in the same recording, or tap Stop to finish and transcribe. To throw the recording away entirely, tap the trash control instead.
