AI Rewrite
Polish what you said into what you meant
How to use it
Open any transcript in Jot and tap Rewrite. Pick one of the built-in prompts, or write your own and reuse it on any transcript with a tap.
Built-in prompts
Cleanup
Removes filler words and fixes grammar while keeping your voice.
Action Items
Pulls the to-dos out of a rambling thought into a clean list.
Email
Formats your notes into a ready-to-send message.
Custom prompt
"Turn this into bullet points," "Translate to French," "Make it more formal." Save it once, reuse anywhere.
On your device
Runs fully on-device
Rewrites use an on-device model (Qwen 3.5 4B). Your text never leaves your iPhone. Enable AI Rewrite in Settings to download the model.
Rewriting is triggered from the transcript, not the keyboard.
