The keyboard

One tap.
You're dictating.

Three controls. The blue one starts the mic. The other two get out of your way. Your recent dictations sit one tap above, ready to re-insert.

  • System-wide custom keyboard — works in any text field on iPhone.
  • Recents stay one tap away. Tap to insert, swipe to delete.
  • Tap the open-in-app icon to take a transcript into the main app for AI rewrite.
In-app recording

Your voice
becomes letters.

Tap Dictate inside the app and the transcript writes itself in editorial type on a Liquid Glass plate. No buttons in the way of your sentence.

  • Stream-in serif transcript — words land as you say them.
  • Five-minute mic. Switch apps mid-sentence and come back.
  • Tap to insert punctuation. The timer lives inside the stop button.
Privacy

Nothing leaves
your iPhone.

Transcription runs on device. AI rewrites run on device. Your voice and your words stay where you put them.

Local speech recognition

Apple's on-device speech models and Parakeet 600M handle transcription. Audio never leaves the device.

Local AI rewrite

Qwen 3.5 4B runs entirely on iPhone 15 Pro and later. Apple Foundation Models fallback for shorter tasks.

No account, no cloud

No sign-in. No server. No third-party SDKs. No analytics that ship your dictations anywhere.

Requires
